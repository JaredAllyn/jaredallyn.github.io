AI Policy

My Commitment to Transparency

Everything on this website — every job title, date, credential, bullet point, and personal detail — is true and accurate. I do not use AI to fabricate, inflate, or misrepresent my experience. My work speaks for itself, and I hold myself to a high standard of honesty in how I present it.

How I Use AI

I built and maintain this website using Claude Code, an AI-powered coding tool developed by Anthropic. I also use Claude as a thinking partner and writing assistant across my work in civic technology and policy communication.

In practice, that means AI helps me with things like structuring content, refining language, writing and debugging code, and iterating on design. Every decision — what to include, how to frame it, what it looks like — came from me. AI is the tool; I am the author.

What AI Did Not Do

AI did not generate my experiences, invent my credentials, or fabricate my achievements. The Peace Corps volunteer work, the PPIA fellowship, the YouTube channel, the certifications, the organizing work — all of it is real and documented. AI helped me present that story clearly, not create it.

Why I'm Transparent About This

I believe AI is a genuinely powerful tool that, when used responsibly, expands what individuals can build and communicate. As someone working at the intersection of technology and civic life, I think it matters that people are honest about how they use these tools — not defensive about it.

Hiding AI use has become a kind of quiet dishonesty in professional life. I'd rather be upfront: yes, I used Claude Code to build this site. Yes, I use AI to help me write and think more clearly. And yes, everything here still reflects my real work, my real values, and my real voice.

My Standard

I use AI as I would any professional tool — to do better work, faster, without compromising integrity. If something on this site is ever inaccurate, that's on me to fix, not the model to answer for.

If you have questions about anything on this site or how I work, feel free to reach out.
