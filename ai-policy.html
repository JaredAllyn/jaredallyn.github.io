AI Policy

Commitment to Transparency

Everything on this website is true and accurate. I do not use AI to fabricate, inflate, or misrepresent my experience. I hold myself to a high standard of honesty in how I present it.

How I Use AI

I built and update this website, in part, using Claude Code, an AI-powered coding tool developed by Anthropic. I also use Claude as a thinking partner across all of my work. In practice, that means AI helps me with things like structuring content, refining language, writing and debugging code, and iterating on design.

What AI Did Not Do

AI did not generate my experiences, invent my credentials, or fabricate my achievements.

Why I'm Transparent About This

I believe AI is a genuinely powerful tool that, when used responsibly, expands what individuals can build and communicate. As someone working at the intersection of technology and civic life, I think it matters that people are honest about how they use these tools.
